Q1 Plan

Tactical Execution

The plan focuses on converting the existing user base and acquiring new leads through progressive discounting. In parallel, we will leverage the Valentine's Day period to increase the average order value through strategic bundles.

January

START

Public Site -20%
Newsletter -10% Extra
Total Members -30%

The goal is to incentivize newsletter subscription by offering an immediate advantage over the public.

Feb 1-14

BUNDLE

Creation of 2-pair bundles based on "Golden Gift" data analysis (products frequently bought together).

We will use a slightly more emotional Tone of Voice for Valentine's Day, positioning the bundle as the perfect gift.

End of February

FLASH

Public Site -30%
Newsletter -10% Extra
Flash Total -40%

Final push to liquidate remaining inventory with an aggressive, time-limited lever.

March

EXTRA

Reserved exclusively for the "Hot Funnel" segment: users subscribed to the "Wheel" (Dec 12-31) who have not yet purchased.

BUNDLE OPPORTUNITY

Technical Pricing Note

It is crucial to calculate the bundle price correctly. To be perceived as a real benefit, the package price (2 pairs) must be lower than purchasing the two products separately applying the current sale discount.

Target Margin: > 5% customer benefit.

SITE OPTIMIZATION (CRO)

We have planned targeted interface interventions to maximize conversion rates during traffic peaks:

Social Proof & Urgency

Insertion of a live sales/view counter on the product page. The goal is to communicate transparently but effectively that products are in demand, reducing user decision time.

Refresh Home Page

Update of main banners to immediately communicate the active phase (Start, Bundle, or Flash) as soon as the user lands on the site.
